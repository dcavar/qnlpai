Why Sponsor?

Quantum AI and NLP brings together researchers, students, and industry partners working at the intersection of quantum computing, AI, and language technologies. Sponsorship helps us support the program, improve visibility for partners, and create a stronger event experience for attendees.

Your support can help fund venue needs, student participation, speaker support, and conference materials while connecting your organization with an active research and innovation community.

Visibility

Reach conference attendees through the website, onsite materials, and event communications.

Community

Support a focused community of academics, practitioners, and students in quantum AI and NLP.

Impact

Help make the conference more accessible, better organized, and more valuable for participants.
