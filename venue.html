Transportation from Indianapolis International Airport to the IU Indianapolis Campus

Transportation from Indianapolis International Airport (IND) to the IU Indianapolis (IUI) campus (~15 mins) is best achieved via Uber/Lyft or taxis, located at the Ground Transportation Center, typically costing $25-$40. For the IUI campus, IndyGo city buses are also available.
Transportation Options to IU Indianapolis (IUI) Campus:

  • Ride-Sharing (Uber/Lyft): Available at the Ground Transportation Center on the first floor of the terminal garage.
  • Taxis: Available at the curb on the lower level of the terminal, just outside Baggage Claim. A $15 minimum charge applies.
  • IndyGo (Public Transit): City buses offer transportation, which may require a short walk to the Indianapolis Marriott Downtown for downtown connections.
  • Car Rental: On-site rental options are available at the airport's Ground Transportation Center.
